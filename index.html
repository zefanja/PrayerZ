PrayerZ

PrayerZ helps you to organize your prayer requests. Enter your different prayers and PrayerZ will sort & combine it for you (based on tags). You can also add notes to a prayer.

Features:

  • Organize your prayers based on tags
  • Add notes to a prayer
  • Prayers with the same title are automatically combined
  • Select the prayers you want to pray for on a weekday
  • easy-to-use interface
